Once you decided on a course you'd like to take, here's the timeline for requesting a course.

Read the Course Description

Look for the course (or some related subject) in our course directory to get a general sense of the topic. You'll see the general description of the course, an example topic, and the suggested 1-on-1 pricing.

Schedule a Consultation

Schedule a free consultation with us! You'll speak to an experienced mentor and organizer for our group, who will help you customize the course you'd like to take and work through logistical plans. If you'd like to act as a referer or schedule a group course, let us know! The hourly price per student can be dramatically decreased.

Take the course!

After the consultation, you'll be able to start your course within a week at the latest. You will be connected with an organizer for the course as well as the tutor themself!

Have a question? Here are common ones we answer!

I need a course specialized for my student. Can you do that?

Yes! Our courses are personalized for their specific student(s). We've taught students with a range of backgrounds, from complete beginners to research-level masters. If you're interested in a topic, schedule a free consultation with us and we'd be happy to elaborate on how to make your class the best it can be.

Are the prices fixed? Are there opportunities for lower prices?

Prices are not fixed—listed prices are suggestions to give a rough idea of our course prices. Taking the class as a small group (2-3 students) instead of 1-on-1 or acting as a referral bringing in students to take other courses can often bring down the listed prices quite a bit. Be sure to talk with us if price is a concern!

Are there opportunities for college counseling or mentorship?

Yes! We regularly handle cases of students looking to apply to top-tier universities and prestigious middle and high schools. From essay ideation to extracurricular aid to standardized testing, we help students reach new heights in their education. Read more about our mentorship and counseling program here. Feel free to reach out to us if you have any questions!
